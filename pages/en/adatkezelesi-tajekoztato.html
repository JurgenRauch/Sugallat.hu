Your personal data is NOT stored or shared with third parties, except in cases necessary for the purpose of contact:

4.1 Contact Data

Data provided through the contact form is stored and used solely for the purpose of communication and correspondence. This data:

  • Is NOT sold or NOT rented out
  • Is used for internal purposes only (quotation preparation, project work, customer service)

4.2 Exceptions (Mandatory Data Transfer)

Your personal data will only be transferred in the following cases:

  • Legal obligation: upon request from authorities or by court order

Data collected by Google Analytics and Facebook Pixel constitutes anonymous statistical information that does not contain personally identifiable data.

5. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

5.1 Right of Access

You have the right to request information on whether we process your personal data, and if so, what data is processed.

5.2 Right to Rectification

You may request the rectification or completion of inaccurate personal data.

5.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to Be Forgotten")

In certain cases, you may request the deletion of your personal data.

5.4 Right to Restriction of Processing

In certain cases, you may request the restriction of the processing of your data.

5.5 Right to Data Portability

You may request to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used format.

5.6 Right to Object

You may object to the processing of your personal data.

5.7 Withdrawal of Consent

You may withdraw your consent to data processing at any time. The withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of data processing carried out on the basis of consent prior to its withdrawal.
