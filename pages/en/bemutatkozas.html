Our Company History

Sugallat Ltd., and its legal predecessor Sugallat '96 Bt., has been in operation since 1996.

Our clients are primarily municipalities, government agencies, civil organizations, and schools, but we also work for businesses and private individuals.

We operate in several seemingly diverse fields, which can be freely combined. We can handle individual parts of your project, or manage it in its entirety. A complete project may include: grant writing for obtaining European Union or Hungarian funding, professional and financial management of the awarded project, design and permitting of the investment's building or buildings, public procurement for construction, services, and equipment, technical supervision of construction, and for contractors, claims management.
