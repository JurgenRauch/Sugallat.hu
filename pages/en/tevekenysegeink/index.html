Our Services

We provide comprehensive professional support to our clients in the fields of public procurement, legal remedies, project management, technical design and grant writing.

Public Procurement for Contracting Authorities

We assist in the lawful and efficient conduct of public procurement procedures, from planning through to contract conclusion.

Public Procurement for Tenderers

We prepare professionally sound, financially competitive and formally flawless tenders for our bidding partners.

Legal Remedies and Dispute Resolution

We provide support in public procurement disputes, dispute resolution and legal remedy proceedings.

Grant Writing and Project Preparation

We increase the chances of your project's success through professional grant writing, from preparation to submission.

Technical Design

Preparation of permit, construction and tender designs, as well as technical support from planning to execution.

Our values and approach

What drives us in our everyday work

Quality and responsibility

Our quality assurance system, compliant with the ISO 9001:2001 standard, ensures the highest level of service. We always assume full legal and financial responsibility for our work.

Long-term partnership

The majority of our clients are returning customers. We strive to deliver our work and set our prices so that you will think of us again next time.

Comprehensive solutions

We operate in several seemingly different fields, yet these can be freely combined. We can manage individual parts of your project, or even the entire project as a whole.

Protecting our clients

We hold professional liability insurance for our public procurement activities. We offer fair compliance with our obligations set out in detailed contracts.
