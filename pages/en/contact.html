Our Office Location

Tárnok, Ősz utca 12.

Our office is located in Tárnok, at the beginning of Ősz Street, which branches off to the right from the Hazatérők Road starting from the castle station, at number 12.

By car:

From Budapest, take the M7 motorway, after passing Érd, after the "Regional Waste Management Center" sign, you need to exit at the Tárnok junction, then leave the roundabout towards Tárnok.

By public transport:

Take the Székesfehérvár line to Tárnok station, then walk along the Hazatérők Road, after the Rákóczi Street intersection, Ősz Street is the 4th street to the right.
