Our Services in Detail

We operate in several seemingly different areas, but these can be freely combined

Public Procurement

Preparation of documents related to public procurement procedures in accordance with the requirements of the Public Procurement Act. For contracting authorities: preparation of procurement documentation, managing the procedure until acceptance, finalizing technical specifications, responding to bidders' questions. For bidders: compilation and review of tenders. For contractors and suppliers: contract review and claims management.

Grant Writing and Project Management

Writing, compiling or reviewing Hungarian and European Union funded grant applications. One or more phases of successful project implementation: professional/financial management, project reporting, liaison with the supporting organization. In exceptional cases, we undertake grant preparation when the complexity of the task or special circumstances justify it.

Technical Design and Supervision

Preparation of building permits, construction and tender plans, as well as investor representation during project implementation, technical supervision, and performing the so-called independent Engineer tasks. Complete building technical design services from permitting to construction.

Environmental Investments

Preparation and management of environmental investments, environmental impact assessment, environmental audits for those required to do so. Comprehensive environmental management consulting and professional support to ensure regulatory compliance.
